Biography

I am currently pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Science at Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Hyeran Byun. I visited Microsoft Research Asia as a research intern, working with Dr. Yan Lu and Dr. Jinglu Wang.

My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, video understanding, and weakly-supervised learning.

I am looking for research groups that I can visit as an intern to collaborate with insightful researchers. Please feel free to contact me.
